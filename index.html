#Deadpool

- The highly anticipated Deadpool movie came out with a huge success with 619.3 million USD box office! The test footage first "leaked" in SDCC 2015 and received huge support by fans. Leading movie studio 20th Century Fox to believe making a rated R Deadpool movie will be successfull (Especially, after last time Deadpool being put on the big screen... let's just say fans arn't too happy with it..). With Ryan Reynolds perfect performance as Deadpool, this "romantic movie" definitely is one of the best movie in 2016.

#Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice

- Comic book fans! This is it! (Have been waiting since the easter egg in "I am Legend"..) After the success of Christopher Nolan's Dark Knight Trilogy and Zack Snyder's Man of Steel, the 2 biggest DC titans is about to clash in this one movie. The idea of Batman and Superman fighting on the big screen is not a dream anymore, basing on the beloved comic book "The Dark Knight Returns" by Frank Miller. As the title stated, this will also be the dawn of the Justice League movie which will be release on 2017.

#Captain America: Civil War

- "The price of freedom is high. It always has been. And it's a price I'm willing to pay. And if I'm the only one, then so be it. But I'm willing to bet I'm not." After the massive success of "Captain America - The Winter Soldier", Marvel Studio is about to complete Captain America's trilogy with Joe and Anthony Russo returning as directors. Although as much as hating to see the Avengers divide, it is very exciting to see Team Cap v Team Tony. (Who side are you on?) Instead of having a conflict over the secret identity like in the comic, looks like it is going to be focusing on the conflict with the Winter Soldier and the events happened in Avengers: Age of Ultron.

#X-Men: Apocalypse

- Bryan Singer returns once again for the final chapter in the trilogy of the X-Men franchise which started strong with "X-Men: First Class". After the huge success of "X-Men: Days of Future Past" proving Bryan still have the interests and amazing talent on directing X-Men films. This time the X-Men will face the largest treat yet, the Apocalypse. He is the biggest, he is the baddest. But no matter, we have some addition young members to the team, including one of fans favourite mutants - Psylocke! "Everything they've built will fall. And from the ashes of their world, we'll build a better one."
